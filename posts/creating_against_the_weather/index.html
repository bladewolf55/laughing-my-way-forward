Making a better life, better late than never

Creating Against the Weather

2023-03-07 19:35

Weather today: gray, cold, and rainy. Which is abnormal.

Mood today: gray, cold, and rainy. Which, given the weather, is normal.

It's not that I can't concentrate when it's overcast, it's that I don't want to. The Sisyphusean effort! It's easier to binge watch a British young adult supernatural show and eat whatever snacks I can drum up.

The thing is, I told my wife I would write today. I didn't want her to come home, ask what I'd done today, and freeze like a lizard on an ice rink. I wanted to make good.


https://www.dreamstime.com/stock-photo-lizard-snow-lat-lacerta-agilis-image52659180

So I did. I wrote a complete short story, something I'd made notes about several days ago. Getting started was the best I hoped for. Finishing--bonus.

If she liked it, my day would be complete. Not that she has to like what I write for me to believe it's good, but it sure does help my confidence.

She liked it.

This entry is for those days the dark cloud tells me "you might as well give up, it's not worth trying, conserve your strength, eat more mustard pretzel rods."

You're wrong.
