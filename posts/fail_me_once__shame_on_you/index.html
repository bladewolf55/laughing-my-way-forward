Fail Me Once, Shame On You

2023-03-06 19:51

What do you do when the person who scheduled your phone interview doesn't call you for a second time?

Well, what I did after fifteen minutes was turn off my headset, resolve to ignore any calls, and applied for the same position at another franchise location.

What will I do if the person contacts me, apologizes, asks to try again? Probably tell the truth.

"You didn't show up for two interviews. That tells me I shouldn't work with you, no matter what the reasons."

Fail me twice, shame on you twice as much, and shame on me if I give you another chance. This is, after all, not a marriage or a child. It's a low-paying job to get my foot in the door and it's not the only door in town.

I also applied to a different job teaching fitness classes for seniors. Since I'm a senior, that's probably a good fit.

On the home front, my wife and I played Bananagrams tonight. I told her and myself and the Cosmos that I didn't expect to win, I wouldn't win, and I didn't win. Her vocabulary and experience at words games is just too much.

It's a good game, but not for us, not for now, not in the "it makes me laugh" kind of way I want to be playing.

We're still on the hunt.

This is my advice. Find a game you enjoy playing with your spouse who is also one of your best friends. Not a game you enjoy winning, but playing. Whatever that means to both of you.
